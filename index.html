Chemistry Concepts

Atomic Structure

The atom is the basic unit of matter. It consists of a dense nucleus surrounded by a cloud of electrons. The nucleus contains positively charged protons and electrically neutral neutrons, while negatively charged electrons orbit the nucleus in energy levels or shells.

Chemical Bonding

Chemical bonding is the process by which atoms combine to form molecules or compounds. The three primary types of chemical bonds are ionic bonds, covalent bonds, and metallic bonds. These bonds determine the properties and behavior of different substances.

Chemical Reactions

Chemical reactions occur when substances interact to form new substances with different properties. These reactions involve the breaking and forming of chemical bonds. Common types of reactions include synthesis, decomposition, single displacement, and double displacement reactions.
